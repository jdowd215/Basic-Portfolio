About Me

I was born on February 15, 1980 and grew up in Issaquah, Washington. After completing high school, I attended California Lutheran University and graduated with a B.S. in Sports Medicine.

Post-college, I worked as an Athletic Trainer in several different settings, including professional baseball with the Boston Red Sox organization. I currently work in business development for a MedTech company and reside on Camano Island with my wife Susan, daughter Isabel, son William, and dog Finn.
